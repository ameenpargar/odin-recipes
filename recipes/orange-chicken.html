Orange Chicken

Orange Chicken with Rice

Home made Orange Chicken with sticky rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Orange Juice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Minced Garlic
  • Grated Fresh Ginger
  • Cornstarch
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Green Onions & Sesame Seeds for Garnish

Steps for Rice

  1. Rinse glutinous rice until water runs clear, then drain.
  2. In a saucepan, combine rice and water.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until rice is tender and water is absorbed.
  4. Serve orange chicken over sticky rice.

Steps for Chicken

  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix together orange juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and cornstarch.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or wok.
  4. Cook chicken pieces until no longer pink.
  5. Pour the orange sauce over the cooked chicken.
  6. Let it simmer until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken.
