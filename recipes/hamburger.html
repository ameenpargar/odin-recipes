Smash Burger

Beef & Triple Cheese

Ingredients

  • Beef Burger
  • Toasted Frisco Bread
  • Creole Dressing
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Pepper Jack Cheese
  • Sweet Onions
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  2. Shape the beef into thin burger patties.
  3. Place a patty on the hot skillet and press it down to "smash" it thin.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side until it's crispy.
  6. Add slices of Cheddar, Swiss, and Pepper Jack cheese to melt during the last minute.
  7. Toast the Frisco bread.
  8. Spread Creole dressing on the toasted bread slices.
  9. Place the cheese-covered patty on one slice.
  10. Top with sweet onions and pickles.
  11. Close the sandwich with the second slice of bread.
  12. Your smash burger is ready to enjoy.
