Pizza Alfredo

Chicken & Veggies Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Chicken
  • Olives
  • Paprika
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Alfredo Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature for your pizza dough (usually around 450Â°F or as indicated on the dough packaging).
  2. Prepare the Pizza Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet that's been lightly greased or lined with parchment paper.
  3. Apply Alfredo Sauce: Spread a generous layer of Alfredo sauce evenly over the pizza dough. Leave a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Layer the Cheese: Sprinkle a layer of cheese (Mozzarella or your choice) over the Alfredo sauce.
  5. Add the Cooked Chicken: Distribute the cooked chicken evenly over the cheese layer.
  6. Add Veggies: Sprinkle the sliced black olives, paprika, sliced onions, and sliced mushrooms over the pizza.
  7. Seasoning: Optionally, you can add extra seasoning like dried oregano, basil, or red pepper flakes for added flavor.
  8. Bake: Carefully transfer the prepared pizza into the preheated oven.
  9. Bake until Golden Brown: Bake the pizza in the oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling and lightly browned.
  10. Cool and Slice: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a couple of minutes.
  11. Slice and Serve: Slice your Chicken & Veggies Pizza Alfredo into portions and serve it hot. Enjoy!
